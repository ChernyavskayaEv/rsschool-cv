My-planning

In this task, an application was developed for planning any cases and goals.

The application allows:

  • Visualize your plans, deeds and goals on boards. Each board for a separate task
  • Each task can be divided into stages of execution in columns (implemented on each board).
  • Each task can be divided into stages of execution in columns (implemented on each board).
  • You can create cards in each column. Cards are your small goals, desires (subtasks) that you want to fulfill in this direction.
  • Each card may (optionally) contain a description or points that you want to highlight. The icon at the bottom of the card indicates the presence of a description.
  • Each card may (optionally) contain a checklist that you mark as you progress. The icon at the bottom of the card indicates the presence of a checklist and the number of items completed.
  • As you work with cards, you can move them through the stages (drag and drop from column to column).
  • Boards (tasks), columns (progress steps), and cards (our subtasks) can be added, edited, removed as needed.
